# Indian-AI-Regulation-Tracker-a-living-GitHub-repository-mapping-AI-emerging-tech-legal-frameworks
An open-access research tracker mapping AI and emerging-tech legal frameworks across India, EU, USA, UK and Singapore — covering DPDP Act 2023, BNS 2023, and the EU AI Act. Annotated case studies, comparative matrices, and research gap analysis. Maintained by Dr. Anjum Hassan, Ph.D. (Law).
